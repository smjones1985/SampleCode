A Mission Statement of sorts:

Modern motorcyles have never appealed to me as much as the ones from my youth. When I began putting together motorcyles, I wanted to harken back to a vintage design age while maintaining a sleek style.

A little about me:

My name is Tom Jones. I was born in California and raised in Arkansas. My whole life I've had a desire to create, build, and design various projects. I have a wonderful wife, four kids, and two grandchildren (with a third on the way!). We currently reside in Amelia, VA.
